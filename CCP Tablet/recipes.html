Double Chocolate Cookies

Description

My daughter learned to make these cookies at a baking camp at Zingerman's and has tweaked the recipe to fit the taste buds of her siblings. They are extremely sugary so the salt helps to balance it. Note, these cookies are best eaten very quickly.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Packed dark brown sugar
  • Large egg
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chunks (whole)
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chunks (melted)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  2. Add the egg and vanilla extract and beat well.
  3. Add the melted chocolate.
  4. In a separate bowl combine the flour, baking soda, cocoa powder and salt.
  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Add the unmelted chocolate chunks.
  7. Form 15 cookies and place on a baking sheet.
  8. Cook for 12 to 13 minutes at 350 degrees.
  9. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before gobbling up!

Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Description

This is a tried-and-tested recipe that I have used numerous times for making the perfect chocolate chip banana bread! Not only does it help utilize the bananas that have slowly overripened at the back of the fridge, it ends up creating an incredibly delicious and relatively "healthy" dessert.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 cups (240 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 large egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup chopped toasted walnuts
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350ยบ. Line a 9"-by-5" loaf pan with parchment and grease with cooking spray.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, mix egg, egg yolk, granulated sugar, butter, sour cream, and vanilla. Add bananas and stir until combined. Gradually add dry ingredients to banana mixture until just combined.
  4. Fold in walnuts and chocolate chips and transfer to prepared pan.
  5. Bake bread until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cool 10 minutes in pan, then invert onto a wire rack and let cool completely.
  6. Consume!

Coca-Cola Chicken Wings

Description

I learned how to cook Coca-Cola chicken wings from my mother, and I've since tweaked her recipe to better suit my family's palate, making it less sweet. While the cooking process remains straightforward, the result is incredibly flavorful, and once you've tried it, you'll understand its delicious appeal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Scallions
  • Ginger slices
  • Cooking wine
  • Dark soy sauce
  • A can of Coca-Cola
  • Toasted sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Wash and place chicken wings in a bowl.
  2. Add scallions, ginger slices, cooking wine, dark soy sauce and salt to the washed chicken wings, and mix them well.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate the chicken wings for 1 hour.
  4. Put the right amount of oil in the pot, and put the marinated chicken wings into the pot and fry it until golden brown on both sides.
  5. After frying the chicken wings until golden brown on both sides, then pour a can of soda into the pan and bring to a boil.
  6. Continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the glaze has thickened and coats the wings nicely.
  7. Sprinkle wings with toasted sesame seeds.
  8. Transfer wings to a serving platter.
  9. Devour!

Swedish Pancakes

Description

Swedish pancakes seem like a difficult recipe, but they're easier to make than you might think! All you need is time, patience, and a sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until completely smooth.Transfer to the refrigerator to rest for at least 2 hours or up to 2 days.
  2. When ready to cook, heat a 10 inch nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Stir the batter briefly to recombine.
  4. Brush skillet with melted butter. Pick the skillet up off of the burner and quickly add 1/4 cup of batter. Immediately begin swirling the batter around the skillet so that it coats the bottom of the pan.
  5. Continue swirling until the batter is just set. Return skillet to the burner. Cook until the pancake is golden brown in spots on the bottom, about 30 seconds.
  6. Use a thin spatula to loosen the pancake, flip it over continue to cook on the other side for about 30 seconds. Transfer to a plate.
  7. Cover with foil and transfer to oven to keep warm and repeat with remaining batter, wiping out skillet in between pancakes with paper towel as needed.
  8. Serve with lingonberry jam and butter or other desired toppings and/or fillings.
  9. Demolish!

BLAT Sandwich

Description

A BLAT sandwich is a delicious twist on the classic BLT, featuring crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and creamy avocado, all sandwiched between slices of bread for a satisfying blend of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. Toast two pieces of bread.
  2. Lightly fry 4-5 slices of bacon.
  3. Slices tomatoes and avocado.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on one slice of toasted bread before putting sliced avocado, sliced tomatoes, bacon, and lettuce between both slices of bread.
  5. Enjoy!
